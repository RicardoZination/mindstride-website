Session 4: Strategies to Improve Quality of Life

Author: Dr. Aroldo Dargél, MD, Ph.D, Chief Medical & Science Officer, Mindstride

Session 4 Overview

In this session, we will identify tips that you can implement into your daily practices that will allow you to improve not only your mental health, but also your overall quality of life. These strategies involve your sleep hygiene, mindful living, and movement/exercise. Let’s get started.

Learning Objectives
  1. Recognize the Benefits of Improving your Quality of Life: Discover how enhancing daily activities can positively impact your quality of life.
  2. Identify Ways to Improve Lifestyle Factors: Increase your awareness of how better sleep, nutrition, and movement can add health benefits and improve your overall mental health.
  3. Create a Personalized Goal Toolkit to Boost your Quality of Life: Develop skills by setting achievable goals tailored to your lifestyle to enhance your quality of life.
Tips for Enhancing Your Daily Activities and Quality of Life​

Balancing your physical, social, mental, and functional health can be overwhelming. Improving your quality of life is a process that takes time and practice. The investments you make toward a better quality of life will benefit your overall health. Here are some suggestions to enhance your daily activities and improve your quality of life and mental health:

Boost Your Sleep Quality

Studies have shown associations between sleep quality and overall quality of life, including increased happiness. Here are some helpful strategies to improve your sleep and quality of life:​

  1. Limit Screen Time Before Bed: Avoid devices at least 1 hour before bed as they emit blue light that can keep you awake.
  2. Prioritize Sleep: Getting enough sleep will improve your mental health, however, if you find yourself not being able to sleep in your bed, we suggest you do something that will promote mindfulness and relaxation to help you wind down from an eventful day.
Boost Your Mood with Mindfulness​

Studies have indicated practicing mindfulness exercises can help you direct your attention away from negative, stressful thinking and engage with the world around you. We suggest these helpful thinking strategies to keep you on track on improving your quality of life:

  1. Live in the moment: Try to intentionally bring an open, accepting and discerning attention to everything you do. Find joy in simple pleasures.
  2. Accept yourself: Treat yourself the way you would treat a good friend.
Boost Your Daily Movement Routine​

Research suggests that physical activity, such as biking or walking, can significantly improve a person’s overall health and quality of life. Here are some helpful strategies to keep you on track:

  1. Incorporate Movement into Your Daily Routine: Find simple ways to add physical activity to your day.​
  2. Set Realistic Goals: Start with small, achievable goals and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your activities.
  3. Find Activities You Enjoy: Choose physical activities that you find enjoyable and fulfilling. Whether it's dancing, hiking, or playing a sport, the key is to make movement a fun part of your day​.
