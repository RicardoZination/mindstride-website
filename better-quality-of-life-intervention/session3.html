Session 3: Managing Quality of Life and Mental Health

Author: Dr. Aroldo Dargél, MD, Ph.D, Chief Medical & Science Officer, Mindstride

Session 3 Overview

In this session, we will delve into the topic of how living with a mental health condition has an effect on quality of life. A mental health condition, regardless of its severity, adds complexity to a person’s day-to-day interactions, support networks, and personal beliefs. Let’s get started.​

Learning Objectives
  1. Increase your Knowledge about Quality of Life: Learn how mental health conditions can affect your perception of quality of life and daily interactions.
  2. Increase your Understanding on the Role of Mental Health in Quality of Life: Increase your ability to recognize the aspects in your life that may be influencing your quality of life if you live with a mental health condition.
  3. Identify the Characteristics of an Improved Quality of Life: Build on your skills and awareness by making informed decisions about the influences you want to improve in your quality of life suited to your lifestyle.
Mental Illness and its Role in Quality of Life​

There are great advantages to improving your quality of life throughout the day that go beyond seeing the benefits in your physical health, such as better heart health by increasing your movement/exercise time. Studies indicate there are also advantages that prevent and manage mental health conditions. People with mental health conditions have noted the aspects below to have a significant role in their quality of life. An improvement in quality of life has been proven to help with their self-perception, relationships, activities, and wellbeing - to name a few of its benefits.

Next Steps

Your mental health and quality of life are linked to self-perception, relationships, autonomy, and hope. Living with a mental health condition adds complexity as symptoms change over time. However, these symptoms might not affect your perception of quality of life if your interactions and daily functioning support your well-being.
