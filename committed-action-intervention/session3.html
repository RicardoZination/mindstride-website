Session Introduction

In Session 3, we delve deep into the practice of stimulus control, a transformative approach that's essential for those struggling to find peace in their nightly routine. It's all about retraining your brain and body to understand that the bed is a cue for Committed Action and nothing else. When you climb into bed, it should signal tranquility and drowsiness, not the frustration of insomnia.

Your path to sleep-friendly habits starts with an intentional bedtime ritual. An hour before your designated Committed Action time, we encourage you to engage in relaxing activities outside of your bedroom. This could be anything that calms the mind, like reading a book, meditating, or listening to soft music. The key is to avoid stimulating activities that could alert your mind, making it harder to wind down.
