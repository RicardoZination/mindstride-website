Session 2: Identifying and Preventing Burnout

Author: Dr. Aroldo Dargél, MD, Ph.D, Chief Medical & Science Officer, Mindstride

Session Summary

In this session, we'll explore the factors contributing to burnout and how they impact your mind and body. You'll take a self-assessment to gauge your burnout levels and learn about the negative effects of burnout on both individuals and organizations. Let’s get started!​

Learning Objectives
  1. Understand Burnout Symptoms: Learn to identify the early signs and symptoms of burnout to maintain your overall wellness at work.
  2. Recognize Behavioral and Emotional Roles: Increase your awareness of specific actions and attitudes that may stem from burnout.
  3. Build Resources for Burnout Early Recognition: Expand your toolkit by completing a self-assessment to measure your level of burnout, helping you understand your current state and recognize early warning signs.

Next Steps

Burnout manifests differently for everyone, and your symptoms can vary daily. As you identify and reflect on your burnout symptoms, consider improving your lifestyle habits to enhance your mental health and prevent or recover from burnout.
