What is Burnout?

The World Health Organization defines burnout as a syndrome, which includes mental and physical exhaustion, resulting from excessive and prolonged stress at the workplace that has not been successfully managed.

Without addressing the root causes, burnout is likely to recur, potentially causing further harm to your mental health and well-being. Ignoring the signs of burnout can lead to more severe consequences.

By recognizing and addressing its early signs, you can prevent burnout from impacting other areas of your life and maintain the energy and ability to meet your job’s demands.
